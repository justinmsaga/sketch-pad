banana bread

the recipe that started it all

Ingredients

mix name

banana
3 banana
1 tsp lemon juice
dry
2 cup flour
1 tsp baking soda
1 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp salt
1 1/2 tsp cinnamon
sugar
1/2 cup white sugar
1/2 cup brown sugar
1/2 cup butter
egg
2 eggs
vanilla
1 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
milk
buttermilk
  • 1/2 tsp vinegar
  • slightly less than 1/2 cup milk
nut
1/2 cup walnut

process

  1. mash banana, add lemon juice [banana] ->
  2. mix flour, baking soda, baking powder, salt, cinnamon [dry] ->
  3. mix both sugar, butter [main] ->
  4. add eggs to [main] ->
  5. add [banana mix] to [main mix] ->
  6. add vanilla to [main mix] ->
  7. add buttermilk to [main mix] ->
  8. add walnut to [dry mix]
  9. fold in [dry mix] into [main mix](do NOT over mix)
  10. bake @325F for 1 hour + time for clear poke stick
  11. cool in pan for 5 mins
  12. cool on rack for 1-24 hrs
